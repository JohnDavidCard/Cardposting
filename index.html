CARDPOSTING. com
personal website of John David Card — writer, artist, historian, and internet dilettante

Hello, and welcome to my website. I like to dabble with all sorts of creative outlets and tinker on personal projects, and I use this space as something of a revolving repository for those I want to publicly showcase. Linked below are the pieces I'd like to spotlight currently, including some short-form fiction, an inventive linguistics exercise, and a couple other resources I've put together recently.

Further down, you'll find some older written material of mine that I still think is worth hanging on to - some informal essays, some poetry, and a few unrealised and shelved projects. At the very bottom of the page are links to where you can find me on other platforms, and an email address you can use to get in touch if need-be.

FEATURED PROJECTS

  • People Mover — a novella about a youngster travelling across the North American prairie
  • 🜽 — a surrealist short story about redundancies in an office
  • Some New Words — a collection of original neologisms
  • Dogwhistles 101 — a video series seeking to explain oblique online reactionary signifiers
  • The Dunwich Reader — a standalone website hosting a wide range of classic horror, adventure, and pulp literature

CAUSERIE

  • Mars Adapts?! — pitching a rework of Steven Spielberg's 2005 War of the Worlds adaptation
  • The The Prisoner Dilemma — demystifying Patrick McGoohan's cult-classic television thriller legacy
  • Beneath the Decline of the Rise of the Reboot of The Planet of the Apes — discussing the devolution of theme and message across the The Planet of The Apes film franchise
