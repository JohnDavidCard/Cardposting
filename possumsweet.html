Possum Sweet

Possum round and possum sweet,
pour some wine while we eat.

Little downy rascal friend,
clambering in my trees again.

tap-tap, rustle, clatter, creak
on feet so swift, and paws so meek.

Top the boughs and make them squeak,
nature's staccato, what a treat.

Possum round and possum sweet,
pour some wine while we eat.
