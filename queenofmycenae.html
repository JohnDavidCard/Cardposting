Queen of Mycenae

Salt the fields of Phrygia,
burn Ilium to the ground.
Toss Astyanx from the battlements,
then tear his fortress down.

Win the slavegirl Briseis,
rip off her wedding gown.
Snatch the diadem of Helen
from Priam's funeral mound.

Agamemnon's decade campaigning,
enshrined in epic song.
But still Homer won't admit -
Clytemnestra did nothing wrong.
